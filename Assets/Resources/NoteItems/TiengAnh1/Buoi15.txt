	A Birthday Party

1. Introduction
- Last Saturday, I attended my best friend’s birthday party at her house.
- The house was decorated with colorful balloons, ribbons, and bright lights.
- Everyone was excited to celebrate and spend time together.

2. Getting Ready
- I bought a small gift, wrapped it in shiny paper, and wrote a birthday card.
- I wore a nice shirt and jeans because I wanted to look good for the party.
- In the afternoon, my parents drove me to her house, where music and laughter could already be heard.

3. Activities at the Party
- First, we sang the “Happy Birthday” song as she blew out the candles on her big chocolate cake.
- We enjoyed delicious food such as pizza, fried chicken, and ice cream.
- After eating, we played fun games like musical chairs and a guessing game.
- Later, we danced to lively music and took many funny photos together.

4. Relaxation and Reflection
- In a quiet moment, I sat with a few friends and talked about school, hobbies, and future plans.
- We laughed a lot and shared stories that made the evening even more memorable.
- I realized how important friendship is and how happy it makes life.

5. Going Home
- Around 9:00 p.m., the party slowly came to an end.
- I thanked my friend’s parents for their hospitality and said goodbye to everyone.
- On the way home, I looked at the birthday photos on my phone and smiled.

6. Feeling
- The birthday party was cheerful, fun, and full of joy.
- I felt lucky to have such good friends and to share special moments with them.
- The evening reminded me that celebrating together strengthens friendship and creates lasting memories.
